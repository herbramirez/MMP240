bio

Carrie Mae Weems is considered to be a huge influential contermporary American artist. She is orignally from Portland, Oregon. She attended multiple universities in California. Carrie has worked and created art through multiple forms with text, fabric, audio, digital, and video. She has explored and been vocal over major social issues through her work. Her work has been featured around the world and most notably at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, Solomon Guggenheim Museum in New York, Centro Andaluz de Arte Contemporaneo in Spain.
